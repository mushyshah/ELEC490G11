Your Survey Results


Thank you for completing the Transferable Learning Orientations (TLO) survey. The data gathered from this survey on your learning dispositions and study skills will provide insights in to your learning, and will help researchers improve the way subjects are taught here at Queen’s. Below you will find a radar chart displaying your results. The “Self-Assessed” score indicates how you perceive yourself in each learning dimension. “Survey Score” shows where you are based on your answers to the survey questions.


The contrast between the two scores highlights differences between how you think your own learning and behaviours measured by each item on the survey.


Further down on the page you will find a description of your results in each category, and what they reveal about your approach to learning. Finally, at the bottom of the page, is a section where you can comment on your results.
